# Social Postify

## üåé Context
With the increasing presence of companies on social media, it is essential to have an efficient tool that allows you to schedule, control and monitor posts on various social media platforms.

## üí† About
"Social Postify" is a web application that allows users to create and schedule posts for various social networks such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn. Users can create personalized posts with images, titles, text and select a specific date and time for each post. The system supports scheduling of multiple posts and provides a clear overview of scheduled posts.

## üõ† Main Features
- Create and schedule posts on various social networks.
- Personalize your posts with images, titles and text.
- Select specific date and time for each post.
- Get a clear overview of all scheduled posts.

## üñá Motivation
The motivation behind the development of Social Postify is to provide an efficient solution for managing social media presence, helping businesses maintain a constant flow of relevant content and reach their audiences more effectively. Automating and scheduling posts can save time and increase the efficiency of digital marketing strategies.
